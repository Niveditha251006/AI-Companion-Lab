# AI-Companion-Lab

 **Helping people learn with AI, not just talk to AI.**

AI Companion Lab is an educational platform that empowers students, developers, and AI enthusiasts to understand, evaluate, and master Artificial Intelligence through interactive tools such as prompt analysis, conversation insights, learning tracking, and responsible AI practices.

Status: Planning 🚀
## 🌱 Project Philosophy

 This AI Companion Lab is not intended to replace human thinking.

Instead, it is designed to help users become better learners, better problem solvers, and more responsible users of Artificial Intelligence by combining AI assistance with critical thinking and continuous learning.
## 🌍 Vision

AI Companion Lab aims to become a complete platform for learning, evaluating, and understanding Artificial Intelligence through interactive tools, analytics, and educational experiences.
